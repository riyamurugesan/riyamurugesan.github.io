About Me



Hi, I’m Riya! I’m a Computer Science and Biology double major at UNC Chapel Hill. I am interested in building models and visualizations that translate complex technical and scientific ideas into clear and accessible visual experiences.

Outside of class, I’ve been exploring 3D modeling and game development to further see how my interests in technology and art intersect. When I’m not troubleshooting on my computer, you’ll find me playing cards and filling my sketchbook!
